INTERVIEW WITH YASMEEN AYOUB

1. What is your dream job and why?
My dream job is to able to apply different fields of design such as graphic design, fashion, and photography into my work and to communicate with clients on an international scale.

2. Did you always know you wanted to major in a design related field or how did you discover you wanted to?
I have always loved design in all of its contexts, whether it be graphic design, fashion, or architecture. I chose Communications Design because I felt like I could explore a broader range of design aspects through this major.

3. Which is your favorite place in the world and why?
My favorite place in the world is my hometown of Kuwait. I grew up there so it's my comfort zone.

4. What's the thing that you love most about New York?
The thing I love most about New York is the endless opportunities that exist here. There is something here for everyone and I love that.

5. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
In 5 years, hopefully I will be getting started on building a name for myself and starting my own design studio in Kuwait.
