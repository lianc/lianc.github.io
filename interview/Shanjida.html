INTERVIEW WITH SHANJIDA KIBRIA

1. Where did you grow up?
Queens, NY.

2. What is your favorite part of being an artist?
How easy it is to focus while creating art.

3. Which of these weekends sounds ideal?
A weekend away with close friends.

4. What time do you usually sleep?
10 or 11pm.

5. What are three things you hate about NYC?
Rats, roaches, prices.

6. And 4 things you love?
There's always something to do, large diverse community, tons of food options, lots of opportunity.
